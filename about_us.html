Our Philosophy

The Compassionate Recovery Center recognizes addiction as a mental health disorder. Therefore, our approach to treatment is the same way we approach any other mental health condition — by treating the emotional, mental and physical symptoms simultaneously while addressing the root cause.

We accomplish this by providing clients with access to addiction experts, personal treatment plans tailored to meet their needs, aftercare programs and other resources for longterm healing. We treat the whole person instead of focusing only on their addiction and we make sure to address underlying conditions that can contribute to substance abuse.

Our long-term treatment plan gives the client time for their brain and body to be able to fight the strong cravings and stay in recovery, and it has been scientifically proven that the longer a client stays in treatment the better they recover. Research indicates that most addicted individuals need at least 3 months in treatment to significantly reduce or stop their drug use and that the best outcomes occur with longer durations of treatment. Our philosophy is that we and the families are all in this together. We understand the wreckage left behind when a loved one is struggling with addiction. To help families heal together, we have developed specific family programming at our facility to psycho educate and help you better and faster.
